# BarrelIQ Label Glossary

These labels are public-facing and should appear directly in the dashboard.

## Weather Edge

The park/weather setup is helping home run conditions.

Typical signals:

- Wind blowing out
- Warm temperature
- Strong HR park factor
- Low air density
- Favorable roof/dome context

## Hot Bat

The hitter has shown recent production, usually over the last 7 to 14 days.

This is context, not the foundation of the pick.

## EV Edge

The hitter's batted-ball quality is strong.

Typical signals:

- Strong average exit velocity
- Strong EV50
- Strong hard-hit rate
- Strong barrel rate

## Pitcher Target

The opposing pitcher allows HR-friendly contact.

Typical signals:

- High barrel rate allowed
- High hard-hit rate allowed
- High xSLG/xISO allowed
- HR issues to the hitter's handedness
- Fly-ball pressure

## Sleeper

The hitter is not the obvious public name but has enough power/matchup support to be relevant.

Typical signals:

- Lower profile
- Lower likely public attention
- Strong matchup or batted-ball edge
- Usually more volatile

## Tough Pitcher

The hitter has power indicators, but the opposing pitcher is not easy to HR against.

This label is not an endorsement by itself. It means the hitter is interesting, but there is pitcher resistance.

## Confirmed

The player is confirmed in the starting lineup.

## Projected

The player is based on projected lineup information and should be treated with caution until confirmed.

## Watch

The player has enough signal to monitor, but not enough to be a core play.
